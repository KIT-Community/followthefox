November 8-10, 2016 | Electric Bookworm

A library tech hackathon

This event was an innovation competition brought to you by the KIT Community and CSU Bakersfield to develop the best technology tool to help with library efficiency and patron access in Kern County that responds to specific needs of local libraries and the community.

Libraries are a place of accessible knowledge, opportunity, and history; a valued community resource. They can also be a place of invention and technology. The KIT Community and CSU Bakersfield invite you to compete to develop needed technologies to help the local libraries better serve the community.

This competition was open to all professions and experience levels. Teams were formed at the event Friday night and worked out innovations throughout the weekend. Students in the fields of computer science, engineering, business, education, and humanities were strongly encouraged to compete.
