Distill Your Idea

A unique engineering competition

BarTech | BrewTech | SafetyTech

Compete to create a cool innovation for bars, breweries, vintners, distilleries, and alcohol safety. Use your engineering, software, business, marketing, or personal experience to revolutionize alcohol in Bakersfield!

Participants will form teams at the competition and work out innovations in a crazy wild weekend marathon of inventing. Teams will be judged on basic prototype, basic business plan, presentation, and overall awe factor.

THEMES: bar technology, brewing technology, and alcohol safety technology

PRIZE: Winning teams will win a super awesome prize like cash or gift certificates and an official award trophy. All participants will get cool swag/promotional items and a participant packet. Some food will be provided.

Must be 21 or older to compete.

If you have any questions, send an email to kerninnovation@gmail.com and use the subject "Distill Your Idea". Interested in volunteering for or sponsoring this event? Let us know at kerninnovation@gmail.com.

This event is sponsored by these local businesses: Mershon Enterprises, MESH Cowork, Creative Cowork, Idea Tailors, Temblor Brewing, Chickadee Entertainment, and Mantis Energy drink.

About the KIT Community

Supporting and Promoting Innovation in Kern County

KIT Community was started in Bakersfield by entrepreneur Alyssa Haerle, who dreamed the dream of all the tech entrepreneurs in town...that some day our tech ecosystem would be thriving and able to provide opportunities for local talent who otherwise leave.

This community seeks to support and promote innovation and technology in Kern County in a movement led by entrepreneurs.

If you agree, follow the fox to a future where Kern County comes to mean innovation and promise to anyone who asks.
